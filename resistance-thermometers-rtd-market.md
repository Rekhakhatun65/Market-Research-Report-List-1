<p><p>Resistance thermometers (RTDs) are temperature sensors that operate on the principle that a material's electrical resistance changes with temperature. The market comprises two main types: thin film resistance thermometers and wire wound resistance thermometers. Thin film RTDs feature a metallic layer that forms a resistive element, offering compactness and rapid response times, making them ideal for general applications. Wire-wound RTDs consist of a wire coil that provides high accuracy and stability, suitable for industrial and high-precision environments.</p></p>
