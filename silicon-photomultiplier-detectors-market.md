<p><p>The Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM) detectors market is divided into two main types: analog and digital. Analog Silicon Photomultipliers offer continuous output signals proportional to the detected light, making them suitable for applications requiring high sensitivity and dynamic range. In contrast, Digital Silicon Photomultipliers provide discrete output signals, enabling precise photon counting and improved temporal resolution. Both types serve various fields, including medical imaging, high-energy physics, and LIDAR systems, enhancing detection capabilities and system performance.</p></p>
