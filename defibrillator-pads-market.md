<p><strong>The Defibrillator Pads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual External</li><li>Manual Internal</li><li>Automatic External</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Defibrillator pads are essential components in cardiac emergency response, categorized into three main types: Manual External, Manual Internal, and Automatic External. Manual External pads are used by trained personnel to deliver shocks externally, while Manual Internal pads are inserted during surgical procedures for direct heart defibrillation. Automatic External Defibrillator (AED) pads are designed for ease of use by laypersons, automatically assessing heart rhythms and delivering shocks as needed, making them widely accessible in public spaces for rapid emergency response.</p></p>
