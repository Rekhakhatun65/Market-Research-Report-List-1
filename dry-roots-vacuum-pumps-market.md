<p><p>Dry Roots vacuum pumps are positive displacement pumps designed for creating high vacuum levels in various industrial applications. They utilize a unique mechanism involving two rotors that rotate in opposite directions, allowing for the efficient evacuation of gases without the need for oil lubrication.
