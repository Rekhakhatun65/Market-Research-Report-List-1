<p><p>The Coastal Surveillance Radar market is segmented into three main types: X-Band Radar, S-Band Radar, and Others. X-Band Radar operates at higher frequencies, providing detailed imaging suitable for smaller targets and close-range detection, making it ideal for coastal and harbor surveillance. S-Band Radar, operating at lower frequencies, offers longer detection ranges and better performance in adverse weather, suitable for larger maritime threats. The "Others" category includes alternative technologies like C-Band and airborne radar systems, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
