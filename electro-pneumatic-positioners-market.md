<p><ul><li>Electro-Pneumatic Single Acting Positioners</li><li>Electro-Pneumatic Double Acting Positioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-pneumatic positioners market is segmented into two main types: single acting and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners utilize pneumatic pressure to move in one direction, typically relying on a spring for return. In contrast, double acting positioners provide motion in both directions using pneumatic pressure, offering greater control and responsiveness. Both types are essential in various industrial applications for precise positioning of valves and dampers, enhancing performance and efficiency in automated processes.</p></p>
