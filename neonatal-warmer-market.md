<p><p>The neonatal warmer market is divided into two primary types: single function and multifunction devices. Single function warmers are designed specifically to maintain a controlled thermal environment for newborns, focusing solely on temperature regulation. In contrast, multifunction neonatal warmers combine temperature control with additional features such as phototherapy, monitoring capabilities, and occasionally resuscitation support. This versatility allows multifunction devices to cater to a wider range of clinical needs, making them increasingly favored in neonatal care settings.</p></p>
