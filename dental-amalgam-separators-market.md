<p><p>Dental amalgam separators are devices used to capture amalgam particles and prevent them from entering wastewater systems. The market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Sedimentation Separators** use gravity to settle heavier amalgam particles at the bottom of a tank for easy removal.</p><p>2. **Centrifugal Separators** utilize centrifugal force to efficiently separate amalgam due to the differences in density, providing a more effective filtration process.</p><p>3. **Other Types** may include hybrid systems or innovative technologies designed for specific dental office needs or regulatory compliance.</p></p>
