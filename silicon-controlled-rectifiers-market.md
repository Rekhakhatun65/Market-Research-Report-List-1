<p><p>The Silicon Controlled Rectifier (SCR) market can be segmented into unidirectional and bidirectional SCR types. Unidirectional SCRs allow current to flow in one direction and are commonly used in applications like phase control and motor speed regulation. In contrast, bidirectional SCRs can conduct current in both directions, making them suitable for alternating current (AC) applications, such as in solid-state relays and control circuits. Both segments cater to diverse industries, including power management, automotive, and renewable energy sectors.</p></p>
