<p><p>The waterborne coating additives market encompasses several key types that enhance the performance of coatings. Wetting and dispersion additives improve the spreadability and uniformity of pigments, ensuring a smooth finish. Defoaming agents eliminate air bubbles during application, preventing surface defects. Rheology modifiers adjust the viscosity and flow characteristics, enabling better control during application. Flow additives enhance the leveling of coatings, reducing imperfections. Together, these additives contribute to improved durability, appearance, and application efficiency in waterborne formulations.</p></p>
