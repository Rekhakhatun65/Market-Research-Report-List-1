<p><p>The Near Infrared Spectrum Instruments market is categorized into three primary types: Filter Type, Dispersion Type, and Fourier Transform Type. Filter Type instruments use optical filters to isolate specific wavelengths for analysis. Dispersion Type instruments break down the light into its spectrum, allowing for detailed composition analysis. Fourier Transform Type instruments utilize mathematical algorithms to interpret signal data, providing high-resolution spectra. Each type serves distinct applications across industries like agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and environmental monitoring, enhancing data accuracy and operational efficiency.</p></p>
