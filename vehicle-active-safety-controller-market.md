<p><strong>The Vehicle Active Safety Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Domain Controller</li><li>Distributed Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the Vehicle Active Safety Controller market, two primary types emerge: Domain Controller and Distributed Controller. Domain Controllers centralize and manage multiple safety functions within a single unit, optimizing communication and reducing complexity. They enhance overall system efficiency but may face limitations in flexibility. Conversely, Distributed Controllers operate independently across various vehicle systems, promoting modularity and scalability. This approach allows for easier upgrades and integration of new technologies, ensuring a responsive and adaptive safety framework for modern vehicles.</p></p>
