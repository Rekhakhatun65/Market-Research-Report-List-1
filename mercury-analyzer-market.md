<p><strong>The Mercury Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mercury Analyzer market is primarily segmented into two types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) Mercury Analyzers and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF) Mercury Analyzers. CVAA analyzers utilize the principle of atomic absorption to detect mercury in samples, offering high sensitivity and specificity. In contrast, CAF analyzers employ fluorescence techniques, providing rapid and accurate measurements with lower detection limits. Both technologies are essential for environmental monitoring, industrial processes, and regulatory compliance, addressing the critical need for mercury detection and quantification.</p></p>
