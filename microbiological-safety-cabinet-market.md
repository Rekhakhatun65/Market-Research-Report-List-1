<p><strong>The Microbiological Safety Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class I Microbiological Safety Cabinets</li><li>Class II Microbiological Safety Cabinets</li><li>Class III Microbiological Safety Cabinets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microbiological safety cabinet market comprises three main types: Class I, II, and III cabinets. Class I cabinets offer basic protection by filtering air before it is released, ensuring personnel safety. Class II cabinets are more advanced, providing a sterile environment and protecting both the product and user through laminar airflow and HEPA filtration. Class III cabinets are fully enclosed, offering the highest level of containment for handling hazardous materials, ideal for high-risk applications in laboratories and research facilities.</p></p>
