<p><p>The automotive plastic coating market comprises three key types: primer, base coat, and clearcoat. Primer serves as an initial layer that enhances adhesion and corrosion resistance, ensuring better surface preparation. The base coat provides color and finishes the visual aesthetic, offering durability and protection. Lastly, the clearcoat adds a transparent layer that shields the base coat from environmental damage, UV rays, and scratches, while enhancing gloss. Together, these coatings contribute to the overall appearance and longevity of automotive components.</p></p>
