<p><p>The Silicon on Insulator (SOI) wafer market is categorized mainly into thick SOI wafers and thin SOI wafers. Thick SOI wafers, typically ranging from 1 to 10 micrometers, are used for high-performance applications like RF devices and power electronics, offering improved isolation and reduced parasitic capacitance. In contrast, thin SOI wafers, often less than 1 micrometer thick, are favored for advanced CMOS technology, enabling better scalability and lower power consumption, making them ideal for mobile and consumer electronics.</p></p>
