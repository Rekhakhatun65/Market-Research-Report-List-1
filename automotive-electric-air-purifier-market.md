<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Air Purifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Electrical Charges Type</li><li>Positive Electrical Charges Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electric air purifier market can be classified into two main types based on electrical charges: Negative Electrical Charges Type and Positive Electrical Charges Type. </p><p>Negative Electrical Charges Type purifiers generate negative ions that attach to airborne pollutants, causing them to clump together and fall out of the air. In contrast, Positive Electrical Charges Type purifiers release positive ions, which attract and neutralize negatively charged particles, helping to cleanse the air. Each type aims to enhance air quality within vehicles, promoting healthier driving environments.</p></p>
