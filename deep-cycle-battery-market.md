<p><p>The deep-cycle battery market is primarily divided into two types: flooded and sealed deep cycle batteries. Flooded deep-cycle batteries use liquid electrolyte, requiring regular maintenance and ventilation, making them ideal for stationary applications like solar energy storage. In contrast, sealed deep cycle batteries, including Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel batteries, are maintenance-free and leak-proof, suitable for various applications such as electric vehicles and portable power solutions. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs based on performance and reliability.</p></p>
