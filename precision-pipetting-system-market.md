<p><p>The Precision Pipetting System market is categorized into two main types: multi-channel and single-channel pipettes. Multi-channel pipettes enable simultaneous dispensing of liquids across multiple samples, improving efficiency in high-throughput applications like drug discovery and clinical labs. In contrast, single-channel pipettes offer precise control for tasks requiring individualized measurement, making them ideal for sensitive experiments and lower-volume applications. Both types cater to specific laboratory needs, enhancing accuracy and streamlining workflows in various scientific and medical fields.</p></p>
