<p><strong>The Automotive Rain Sensing Wiper Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Sensor</li><li>Capacitive Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive rain sensing wiper systems market is primarily divided into two types: optical sensors and capacitive sensors. Optical sensors detect raindrops on the windshield by measuring changes in light reflection, allowing for precise wiper activation and speed adjustment. In contrast, capacitive sensors monitor changes in capacitance caused by water on the glass surface, enabling activation when moisture is present. Both technologies aim to enhance driving safety and convenience by automating wiper operations in response to varying rain conditions.</p></p>
