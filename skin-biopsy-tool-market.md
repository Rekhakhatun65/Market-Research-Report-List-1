<p><p>The skin biopsy tool market is categorized into three main types: biopsy punch, biopsy blade, and others. Biopsy punches are cylindrical tools used to remove cylindrical sections of skin for testing, often utilized for their minimal invasiveness. Biopsy blades, typically resembling surgical scalpels, are employed for excisional or incisional biopsies, allowing for larger tissue samples. The "others" category includes specialized tools like curettes and dermal curettes, catering to various dermatological needs and procedures, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and patient care.</p></p>
