<p><p>The pH sensor cartridge market is divided into two main types: single junction and double junction pH sensor cartridges. Single junction cartridges feature a single reference electrode, providing a simpler design suitable for many general applications. In contrast, double junction cartridges utilize two reference electrodes, enhancing stability and accuracy, particularly in challenging environments with potential interference or high contamination. Both types cater to diverse industrial, agricultural, and laboratory needs, ensuring precise pH measurements critical for various processes.</p></p>
