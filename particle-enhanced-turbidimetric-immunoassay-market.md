<p><p>Particle Enhanced Turbidimetric Immunoassay (PETIA) is a sophisticated analytical technique that combines immunoassay principles with turbidity measurement to quantify specific antigens or antibodies in a sample. This method utilizes the aggregation of particles coated with antibodies, which leads to an increase in turbidity when the target analyte is present. PETIA offers high sensitivity and specificity, making it suitable for a variety of applications, including clinical diagnostics, environmental monitoring, and food safety.</p><p>The Particle Enhanced Turbidimetric Immunoassay Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.50% during the forecast period.
