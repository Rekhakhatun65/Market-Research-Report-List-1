<p><p>Equine insurance encompasses various market types to suit different needs. Lifetime cover provides continuous health protection, ensuring treatments for chronic conditions are covered throughout the horse's life. Non-lifetime cover offers protection only for conditions existing during the policy term, ceasing once the policy ends. Accident-only insurance covers injuries resulting from accidents but excludes illnesses. Other market options may include specialized policies tailored for specific breeds, activities, or usage, providing flexibility based on the horse's lifestyle and needs.</p></p>
