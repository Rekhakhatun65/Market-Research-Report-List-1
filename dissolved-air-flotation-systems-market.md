<p><p>Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF) Systems are innovative water treatment technologies that utilize the principle of flotation to separate suspended solids from liquids. By dissolving air in water under pressure and then releasing it at atmospheric pressure, tiny air bubbles adhere to solid particles, causing them to rise to the surface for removal. This method is particularly effective in treating wastewater in industries such as food and beverage, oil and gas, and municipal applications.</p><p>The Dissolved Air Flotation Systems Market is poised for significant growth, expected to increase at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14.4% during the forecast period.
