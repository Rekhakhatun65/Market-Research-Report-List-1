<p><strong>The ECG Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Holter Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECG monitoring system market is categorized into three main types: Resting ECG, Stress ECG, and Holter Monitors. Resting ECG captures the heart's electrical activity at rest, providing essential baseline data. Stress ECG, conducted during physical exertion, evaluates heart performance under stress to identify potential issues. Holter Monitors are portable devices worn for 24-48 hours, continuously recording heart rhythms to detect irregularities that may not appear during brief tests, thus offering comprehensive insights into cardiac health.</p></p>
