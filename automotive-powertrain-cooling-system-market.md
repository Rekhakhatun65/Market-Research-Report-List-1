<p><ul><li>ATOC</li><li>ATF</li><li>ITOC</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive powertrain cooling system market consists of several key types: </p><p>1. ATOC (Air-to-Oil Coolers) utilize air to cool engine oil, enhancing performance and longevity. </p><p>2. ATF (Automatic Transmission Fluid Coolers) regulate the temperature of transmission fluid, ensuring smooth operation and preventing overheating. </p><p>3. ITOC (Intercoolers for Turbocharged Engines) cool air compressed by the turbocharger, improving engine efficiency and power output. </p><p>4. Others include various specialized cooling solutions designed for unique automotive applications, contributing to overall vehicle efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
