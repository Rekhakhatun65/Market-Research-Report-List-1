<p><p>Electric injection machines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types, each serving distinct manufacturing needs. Vertical injection machines utilize a vertical clamping mechanism, ideal for small, intricate parts and applications requiring minimal space. In contrast, horizontal injection machines provide a robust framework suited for larger components and high-volume production, facilitating faster cycle times. Both types enhance efficiency, precision, and energy savings in the injection molding process, making them essential in diverse industries, from automotive to consumer products.</p></p>
