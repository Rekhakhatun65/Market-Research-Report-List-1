<p><p>The urine analyzer paper market is categorized into two main types: monolayer and multilayer structures. Monolayer structure consists of a single layer of reactive chemicals that changes color in response to specific urine components, providing a straightforward analysis. In contrast, multilayer structure features multiple layers, each designed to react to different substances, allowing for a more comprehensive evaluation of urine samples. The multilayer type generally offers enhanced sensitivity and a broader scope of analysis, making it popular in clinical settings.</p></p>
