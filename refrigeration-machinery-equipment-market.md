<p><strong>The Refrigeration Machinery & Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Refrigeration Machine</li><li>Absorption Refrigeration Machine</li><li>Steam Jet Refrigeration Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigeration machinery and equipment market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Compression Refrigeration Machines** utilize mechanical compressors to vaporize refrigerants, efficiently removing heat and providing cooling for various applications.</p><p>2. **Absorption Refrigeration Machines** rely on heat sources, like gas or solar energy, to drive the cooling cycle, using a refrigerant and absorbent system, ideal for remote locations or waste heat recovery.</p><p>3. **Steam Jet Refrigeration Machines** use high-pressure steam to create a vacuum, enabling the refrigeration cycle, making them suitable for specific industrial applications where steam is readily available.</p></p>
