<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Mechanical Pretensioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buckle Pretensioner</li><li>Retractor Pretensioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive mechanical pretensioner market comprises two primary types: buckle pretensioners and retractor pretensioners. Buckle pretensioners are integrated into the seatbelt buckle, tightening the belt in the event of a collision, effectively reducing occupant movement. Conversely, retractor pretensioners are located within the seatbelt retractor, automatically pulling the belt tight during an impact. Both types enhance passenger safety by minimizing slack in the seatbelt, thereby improving protection during an accident and contributing to overall vehicle safety systems.</p></p>
