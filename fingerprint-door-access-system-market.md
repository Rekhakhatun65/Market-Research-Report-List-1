<p><p>The Fingerprint Door Access System market can be categorized into several types based on the technology used. Optical systems capture high-resolution images of fingerprints using light, providing accurate identification. Capacitive systems utilize electrical signals, sensing the unique characteristics of a fingerprint's ridges and valleys for verification. Ultrasonic systems emit sound waves to create a three-dimensional profile of the fingerprint, enhancing security. Other technologies may include thermal or RFID-based systems, each offering varying degrees of precision and application versatility.</p></p>
