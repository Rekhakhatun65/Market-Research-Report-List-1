<p><strong>The Active Optical Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simplex Active Optical Connectors</li><li>Duplex Active Optical Connectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Optical Connectors market is categorized into two primary types: Simplex and Duplex Active Optical Connectors. Simplex Active Optical Connectors transmit data in a single direction, making them ideal for applications requiring one-way communication, such as sensor networks. In contrast, Duplex Active Optical Connectors facilitate bidirectional data transmission, supporting both incoming and outgoing signals simultaneously, which is essential for high-speed data transfer in environments like data centers and high-performance computing. Both types enhance flexibility and efficiency in optical communication systems.</p></p>
