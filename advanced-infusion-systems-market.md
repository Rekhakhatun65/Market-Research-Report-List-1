Intravenous sets facilitate the connection between the pump and the patient, while IV cannulas serve as temporary access points for fluid administration. Needleless connectors enhance safety by allowing the connection and disconnection of tubing without needles, reducing the risk of infection and needlestick injuries in healthcare settings.</p></p>
