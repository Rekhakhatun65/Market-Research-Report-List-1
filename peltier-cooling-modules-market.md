<p><strong>The Peltier Cooling Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Modules</li><li>Multi-stage Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peltier cooling modules market comprises single-stage and multi-stage modules. Single-stage modules utilize a single thermoelectric element to create a temperature differential for cooling applications, making them ideal for compact spaces and lower cooling requirements. In contrast, multi-stage modules stack several single-stage units, enabling greater temperature differentials and enhanced cooling capabilities, suitable for demanding applications in medical devices and high-performance electronics. Both types cater to varying thermal management needs across diverse industries.</p></p>
