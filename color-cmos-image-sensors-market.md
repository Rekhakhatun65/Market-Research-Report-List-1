<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Color CMOS Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Global Shutter</li><li>Rolling Shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Color CMOS Image Sensors market is primarily divided into two types: Global Shutter and Rolling Shutter. Global Shutter sensors capture all pixels simultaneously, effectively eliminating motion distortion, making them ideal for high-speed imaging in applications like industrial and automotive. In contrast, Rolling Shutter sensors read out pixels sequentially, which can introduce distortion during rapid movement, but they are cost-effective and widely used in consumer electronics like smartphones and webcams. Each type addresses specific needs within the imaging market.</p></p>
