<p><p>Resectoscopes are specialized medical instruments used primarily in urology and gynecology for tissue removal and resection procedures. The market is categorized into unipolar and bipolar resectoscopes. Unipolar resectoscopes utilize a single electrode, allowing for effective resection in saline solutions, while bipolar resectoscopes employ two electrodes, which are safer in high-risk patients by reducing electrosurgical burns and enhancing hemostasis. Both types are essential for minimally invasive surgeries, with applications expanding in various medical fields.</p></p>
