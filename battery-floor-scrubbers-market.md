<p><p>The battery floor scrubbers market includes three primary types: walk-behind, ride-on, and stand-on floor scrubbers. Walk-behind floor scrubbers are compact and manually operated, ideal for smaller spaces. Ride-on floor scrubbers allow operators to sit while cleaning larger areas efficiently and comfortably. Stand-on floor scrubbers provide a middle ground, offering operators a standing position with greater mobility compared to walk-behind models, suitable for medium-sized spaces. Each type caters to different operational needs based on facility size and cleaning requirements.</p></p>
