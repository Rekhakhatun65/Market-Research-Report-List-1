Solid Phase Bonding Technology allows for the bonding of different materials without excess heat, improving reliability. LIGA Technology combines lithography, electroplating, and molding to produce precise, high-aspect-ratio microstructures, ideal for high-frequency applications in RF devices.</p></p>
