<p><p>The electro-mechanical brake market comprises various types designed for diverse applications. Single face brakes feature a single contact surface, ideal for compact spaces. Power-off brakes engage without power, enhancing safety in emergencies. Particle brakes utilize magnetic particles for smooth braking, suited for precise control. Hysteresis power brakes leverage magnetic forces for consistent performance. Multiple disk brakes combine several friction surfaces to provide high torque in compact designs, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications where space and efficiency are critical.</p></p>
