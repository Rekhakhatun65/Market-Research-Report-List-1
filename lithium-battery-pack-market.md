<p><p>The lithium battery pack market includes several types, each with distinct characteristics. Lithium Cobalt Oxide (LCO) offers high energy density, ideal for consumer electronics. Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt Oxide (NMC) balances power and energy, used in various applications. Lithium Nickel Cobalt Aluminum Oxide (NCA) delivers high performance for electric vehicles. Lastly, Lithium Titanate (LTO) provides rapid charging and long cycle life, suitable for high-power applications.</p></p>
