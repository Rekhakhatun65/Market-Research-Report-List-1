<p><p>The Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) market comprises several technologies: </p><p>1. **RFID-Based ETC Systems** utilize radio frequency identification to automatically charge tolls as vehicles pass through gantries. </p><p>2. **DSRC-Based ETC Systems** employ dedicated short-range communications for fast, reliable transactions between vehicles and toll facilities. </p><p>3. **Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems** use cameras and image processing to read license plates for toll collection. </p><p>4. **GNSS/GPS-Based ETC Systems** leverage satellite positioning for dynamic road usage charges based on vehicle location. Each system caters to different operational needs and infrastructure capabilities.</p></p>
