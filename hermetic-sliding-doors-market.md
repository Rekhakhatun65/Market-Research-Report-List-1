<p><p>Hermetic sliding doors come in two primary market types: single leaf and double leaf. Single leaf doors consist of a single panel that slides open, ideal for spaces requiring minimal access while maintaining airtight sealing. They are often used in laboratories and cleanrooms. Double leaf doors feature two panels that slide apart, allowing wider openings, making them suitable for high-traffic areas like hospitals and pharmaceutical facilities. Both types ensure effective environmental control, crucial for maintaining sterile and secure conditions.</p></p>
