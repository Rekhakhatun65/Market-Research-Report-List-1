<p><p>The hydraulic patient lift market primarily includes three types: hand crank, push lever, and others. Hand crank lifts utilize a manual crank to raise the patient, offering simple operation without electrical components. Push lever lifts allow caregivers to easily lift and lower patients with minimal effort, enhancing safety and comfort. Other types may incorporate advanced features like power assist or remote control. Each type caters to different care environments, emphasizing convenience, efficiency, and patient safety in handling mobility assistance.</p></p>
