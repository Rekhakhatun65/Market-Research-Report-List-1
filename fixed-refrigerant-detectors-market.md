<p><strong>The Fixed Refrigerant Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halide</li><li>Electronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed refrigerant detectors are essential for monitoring refrigerant leaks in commercial and industrial settings. They primarily come in two types: halide and electronic detectors. Halide detectors utilize gas-sensitive halide compounds that change color in the presence of refrigerants, providing a visual indication of a leak. In contrast, electronic detectors employ advanced sensors to detect refrigerant concentrations, offering precise measurements and often integrating with alarm systems for immediate alerts. Both types play a critical role in ensuring safety and compliance within facilities.</p></p>
