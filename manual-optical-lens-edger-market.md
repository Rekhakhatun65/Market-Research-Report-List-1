<p><strong>The Manual Optical Lens Edger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mould Lens Edger</li><li>Mould Free Lens Edger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual optical lens edger market comprises two main types: mould lens edgers and mould-free lens edgers. Mould lens edgers utilize pre-made moulds to shape and grind lenses, ensuring high precision and consistency, making them suitable for mass production of prescription eyewear. In contrast, mould-free lens edgers allow for more versatility as they can handle various lens shapes and prescriptions without the need for specific moulds, catering to custom or specialty lens applications. Both types serve essential roles in optical laboratories and retail optical shops.</p></p>
