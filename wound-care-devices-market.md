<p><p>Wound care devices encompass various products designed to promote healing and protect wounds. Foam dressings absorb exudate and provide cushioning, while hydrocolloids create a gel-like environment to aid healing. Alginates, derived from seaweed, are used for highly exuding wounds, and transparent films offer a protective barrier while allowing visibility. Hydrofiber dressings manage excess fluid, and hydrogels hydrate dry wounds. Collagen dressings support tissue regeneration, while "others" include advanced technologies such as bioengineered skin and negative pressure wound therapy.</p></p>
